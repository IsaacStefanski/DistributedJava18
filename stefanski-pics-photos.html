Auto Shows

The newest, sleekest rides are on display for everyone to see when Auto Show Season rolls in! Here are some of the best of the best from the Chicago and Milwaukee Auto Shows.

Cool Cars

Fast. Furious. Luxurious. Driving alongside a Ferrari or Maserati, or passing by a rare car in a parking lot is a pretty awesome moment. Other times it's admiring automotive history at places like the Wisconsin Auto Museum in Hartford or the Volo Auto Museum in Illinois.

The City

Sometimes you just have to capture that neat skyline or the picturesque architecture downtown.

Food

Presentation matters. You eat with your eyes! Anything from fine dining to a display of handmade confections, if it looks good, well, let's just say your stomach is waiting.

Out and About

You could see anything, anywhere, anytime. It could be something eye-catching you pass by, it could be a monument, it could be something you don't know if you'll ever see again. Sometimes the best pictures are the unplanned shots when your just out on the town.

Outdoors

The photo opps are unlimited when you're outside. The lake, a forest up north, the zoo, a state park, even your own yard can be quite the destination for a photo shoot and just having fun behind the camera.
